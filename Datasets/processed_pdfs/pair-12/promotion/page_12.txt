What is Recorlev?
* Recorlev is a prescription medicine used to treat high cortisol (endogenous hypercortisolemia) levels in adult patients with Cushing's syndrome who cannot have surgery or who have had
  surgery that did not cure their Cushing's syndrome.
* It is not known if Recorlev is safe and effective for the treatment of fungal infections. Recorlev is not to be used for treatment of fungal infections.
